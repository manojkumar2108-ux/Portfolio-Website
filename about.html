About Me

Developer · Designer · Accessibility Advocate

Hello, world! 👋

I'm Manojkumar, a front-end developer and UI/UX designer with over six years of experience building products that are beautiful, fast, and accessible to everyone — regardless of ability or device.

My journey started with a fascination for how the web works: the moment I learned I could build something in a text editor and open it in a browser was genuinely life-changing. Today, I specialise in translating complex design systems into clean, maintainable code, and I'm deeply passionate about WCAG compliance and inclusive design.

When I'm not writing code or pushing pixels, you'll find me hiking trails around the Bay Area, contributing to open-source accessibility projects, or perfecting my pour-over coffee technique.
